Information on Allergens


We at Bakery are committed to exceptional standards of quality and kitchen organization. However, as we are a small shop, all of our equipment is shared. In addition to dairy and wheat products, other ingredients that may be of concern to those with food allergies include, but are not limited to, tree nuts (almonds, pistachios, hazelnuts, walnuts and pecans), pinenuts, and peanuts.


Please do not hesitate to ask if you have a question or concern about a particular product.
